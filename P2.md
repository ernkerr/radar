## Scrape Customer Website for Product Data
- Use TinyFish to scrape a customer's website and auto-populate their product catalog
- Could pull species, origins, formats, certifications from their public product pages
- Reduces manual onboarding effort significantly

## Custom Source Monitoring
- Let companies add their own URLs to watch (e.g., supplier websites, industry news, custom regulatory pages)
- Requires adding `company_id` to `sources` table (currently global-only)
- Could make onboarding easier — "paste the URLs you currently check manually"
- Need to decide: separate `custom_sources` table vs adding optional `company_id` to existing `sources`
